About

Dragon Calculator was created with one simple idea in mind: everyday calculations shouldn't need a cluttered, ad-heavy, or confusing app. We wanted a calculator that opens instantly, looks clean, and gets out of your way so you can focus on your numbers.

This tool was built for students, shopkeepers, professionals, and anyone who needs quick, reliable calculations without unnecessary distractions. It supports all the basic operations you use every day — addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division — along with handy extras like percentage calculations and quick sign changes.

Dragon Calculator is designed with a minimal dark theme that's easy on the eyes, whether you're using it during the day or late at night. We believe good tools don't need to be complicated, and we're committed to keeping this calculator simple, fast, and free to use for everyone.
