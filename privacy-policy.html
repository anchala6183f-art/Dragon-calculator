Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy outlines how Dragon Calculator ("we", "our", or "the site") processes, handles, and protects information when you visit, interact with, or utilize our web application. We are deeply committed to ensuring transparency, privacy, and data security for all our global users.

2. Information We Collect

Dragon Calculator is built with privacy at its core. We do not require any user account registration, sign-up, or profile creation. We do not collect or request any personally identifiable information (PII) such as names, physical addresses, telephone numbers, or location histories to access the calculator features.

All calculations, math operations, and input histories are computed and processed entirely inside your local device's web browser memory. This data is never sent to our servers, never stored remotely, and is completely wiped whenever you close your browser tab or clear your site cookies.

3. Cookies and Advertising (Google AdSense)

This website displays advertisements served by Google AdSense to fund our operations and keep our tool completely free of charge. Google AdSense uses cookies, device identifiers, and similar technologies to gather anonymous traffic data and deliver ads that are tailored to your interests:

  • Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website or other websites on the internet.
  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to our users based on their visit to this site and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's advertising control dashboard at www.google.com/settings/ads, or by using general browser blocker tools and cookie preferences.

Additionally, you can visit the Network Advertising Initiative (NAI) opt-out page at optout.networkadvertising.org to disable third-party personalized advertising cookies across multiple ad networks.

4. Standard Log Files and Analytics

Like almost all websites, Dragon Calculator uses standard automated log files. These files record non-personal information when you access the site. This includes: Internet Protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date/time stamps, referring/exit pages, and the number of clicks. This data is utilized solely for analyzing overall traffic trends, administering the site, tracking generic user movement, and ensuring server stability. It is never linked to any personally identifiable information.

5. GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

For users residing within the European Economic Area (EEA), we comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). In accordance with GDPR guidelines:

  • We serve non-personalized ads by default to users who have not explicitly consented to personalized cookies.
  • You retain the right to deny or withdraw consent for third-party tracking cookies at any time via your browser settings.
  • No personal files, emails, or location coordinates are ever extracted or stored by us.

6. CCPA Compliance (California Users)

For users residing in California, USA, we adhere to the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). California residents have the right to:

  • Request disclosure regarding what categories of personal information we have collected (we do not collect personal information).
  • Request deletion of any personal data stored (since no data is sent to our servers, we hold no records to delete).
  • Opt-out of the "sale" of personal data (including personalized ad networks). You can do this by managing your browser tracking cookie permissions or via Google's Ad Settings.

7. Children's Privacy

Protecting the privacy of children is of paramount importance to us. Dragon Calculator is a general-utility mathematics calculator and does not knowingly collect, store, or process any personal data from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided us with any data, please contact us immediately, and we will take instant steps to ensure its removal.

8. Third-Party Links

Our website contains advertisements and external web links. We do not control, review, or assume responsibility for the privacy practices, content, or cookie policies of any third-party websites. We encourage our users to read the privacy statements of any external site they visit.
